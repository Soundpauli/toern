Settings

How the grid behaves — lighting, page walking, paint style, cursor, LEDs, and PONG. None of these change the audio engine; they shape interaction and visuals only. Open from the root menu as SETTINGS; encoder 4 walks the eleven leaves below.

Entering SETTINGS

From the root menu, highlight SETTINGS and short-press encoder 4. Row 16 re-colours to the SETTINGS family tint so you always know you are inside this world. Encoder 4 turn flips siblings; encoder 1 short steps back to the root list.

SETTINGS family entry on the root menu.

PONG — bouncing retriggers

A ball bounces across the grid in draw mode. Every time it lands on a cell that already has a note, that note fires again.

PONG on: speed readout left, ON right, ball on the middle rows.
